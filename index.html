Shinnam Yoo

Exploring the Hidden World of Mycorrhizal Fungi

My research goal is to understand how fungi interact with their environments and to generate knowledge that can support carbon sequestration and climate change mitigation. Through my work on Tricholoma matsutake, I also developed an interest in sustainable agriculture. I also aim to promote public appreciation of fungi through education inspired by my wild mushroom foraging.
