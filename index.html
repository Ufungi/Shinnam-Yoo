About Me

I have a passionate interest in various aspects of fungi. Currently, my research focuses on the microbiome and metabolism of one of the most prized mushrooms, Tricholoma matsutake. I hope my research and teaching will one day contribute to biodiversity conservation and preventing climate change. In my free time, I enjoy foraging mushrooms and watching birds.
