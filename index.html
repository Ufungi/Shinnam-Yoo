Research Focus

üî¨ Ectomycorrhizal Ecology & Microbiome

Investigating core microbiomes of ectomycorrhizal fungi through cross-study phylogenetic analysis and metabarcoding. Specialized in Tricholoma matsutake holobiont systems, fine-scale community assembly, and spatial distribution patterns in forest ecosystems.

üß¨ Multi-Omics & Systems Biology

Integrating transcriptomics, metabolomics, and genome-scale metabolic modeling to understand fungal physiology and microbe-environment interactions. Development of bioinformatics pipelines for heterogeneous amplicon sequence analysis using phylogenetic approaches.

üå≤ Conservation & Sustainable Agriculture

Bridging fundamental mycology with climate change mitigation through carbon sequestration research. Developing cultivation technologies for valuable mushroom species and promoting public appreciation of fungi through education and wild mushroom foraging.
