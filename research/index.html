Microbiome · 2026

Functionally distinct core microbes of Tricholoma matsutake revealed by cross-study analysis

Yoo, S., Seo, C. W. & Lim, Y. W.

Cross-study meta-analysis of T. matsutake microbiomes across diverse geographic and environmental contexts reveals a functionally distinct set of core bacterial associates. Phylogenetic placement and functional annotation indicate consistent ecological roles within the holobiont, suggesting co-evolutionary relationships between the mushroom and its microbial partners.

Mycorrhiza · 2022

Exploring Fine-Scale Assembly of Ectomycorrhizal Fungal Communities Through Phylogenetic and Spatial Distribution Analyses

Yoo, S., Cho, Y., Park, K. H., & Lim, Y. W.

Integrates phylogenetic and spatial approaches to examine fine-scale community assembly of ectomycorrhizal fungi in Korean forest ecosystems. Results reveal significant phylogenetic signal in spatial distribution, highlighting the roles of host specificity and dispersal limitation in structuring ECM communities at local scales.

Mycobiology · 2022

Fourteen Unrecorded Species of Agaricales Underw. (Agaricomycetes, Basidiomycota) from the Republic of Korea

Yoo, S., Cho, Y., Kim, J. S., Kim, M., & Lim, Y. W.

Documents fourteen new records of Agaricales for the Republic of Korea, based on morphological characterization and molecular phylogenetic analysis. Each species is described with diagnostic features, ecology, and distribution, expanding the known macrofungal diversity of East Asia and providing a foundation for future ecological and taxonomic studies.
